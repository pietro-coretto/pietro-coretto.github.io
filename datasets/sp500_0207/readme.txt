********************************************************************************
DATA SET: 	SP500 2002-2007
********************************************************************************

The data set contains adjusted closing prices for a selection  of 100  stocks 
traded on the S&P500 market from 2/Jan/2002 to  31/Dec/2007.  

Columns  are as follows

Col 1:		calendar dates
Col 2:		S&P500 prices
Col 3-102:	prices of a selection of stocks

Stocks' code-name are given as  column labels.  There are calendar 
dates for which  prices have not been recorded because not all stocks 
are traded for the whole sampling period. These are reported 
as NA records.
