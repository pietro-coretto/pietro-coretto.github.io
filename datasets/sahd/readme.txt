********************************************************************************
DATA SET: South African Heart Disease
********************************************************************************

Contains a sample of males in a heart-disease high-risk region of the Western
Cape, South Africa. These data are taken from a larger dataset, described in
detail in Rossouw et al (1983). The data set has been originally obtained
from the public repository
                 https://web.stanford.edu/~hastie/ElemStatLearn/
and processed for teaching purposes. Examples of applications are given in
Hastie et al. (2009)


* Sampling: cross-section

* Sample size: 462

* Number of variables: 10

* Container: comma-separated values (csv) format file 

* Variables 
  chd	     coronary heart disease, =1 if chd present, =0 otherwise 
  sbp	     systolic blood pressure
  tobacco    cumulative tobacco (kg)
  ldl	     low densiity lipoprotein cholesterol
  adiposity
  famhist    family history of heart disease (Present, Absent)
  typea	     type-A behavior
  obesity
  alcohol    current alcohol consumption
  age	     age at onset
