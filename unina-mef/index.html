Program and course structure

The course covers the pillars of modern econometrics at an intermediate/advanced level. There will be a total of 28 hours of theory/methodology classes, plus 8 hours of computing labs during which the student is exposed to the basics of the R computing language. The student is expected to build upon this base knowledge by working on two take-home project works. The latter is an essential ingredient of the course, and it is used as work bench accompanying the development of the class lectures. A strong emphasis is given to the analysis and processing of real modern large data sets.
