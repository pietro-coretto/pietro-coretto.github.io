Pietro Coretto
Professor of Statistics

Short Bio. I am full professor of Statistics at the University of Salerno and a faculty member of the NSE graduate school of the University of Naples "Federico II". I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from University College London (UCL, UK), and my Master of Science (M.Sc.) at the University of Pennsylvania (UPenn, USA). I am broadly interested in the intersection of statistics and computational data science, with applications to machine learning and artificial intelligence. Over the years, I have taught both undergraduate and graduate courses in inference, probability, computational and learning methods, and econometrics. For more information, see the CV linked below.
